Lockit

A stylish wearable that blends fashion, function and technology to keep you from losing or misplacing your smartphone!

What is LOCKIT?

  • The Lockit is worn on the purse or keychain
  • It's a smart device that prevents you from losing or misplacing your smartphone
  • Interchangeable skins match your different purses, styles or outfits

The Fashion Accessory Redefined

  • Set the trend and accessorize your purse with the Lockit
  • Wear it your way with a variety of simple and beautiful designs
  • Make a statement and express yourself with the Lockit
  • Match your Lockit with different purses and outfits and be alerted if your smartphone is left behind

How it Works?

  • The Lockit uses Bluetooth low-energy technology and creates a virtual leash with your smartphone
  • Beep! Beep! Beep! The Lockit alarms you with an LED and audio alert if your smartphone is left behind
  • Can't find your smartphone - maybe lost in your purse? Simply tap the Lockit and your smartphone alarms
  • Wear the Lockit on your misplaced keys and page them from the smartphone app
