What is LOCKIT?

  • The Lockit is worn on the purse or keychain
  • It keeps you from losing or misplacing your smartphone
  • Interchangeable cases match your different purses, styles or outfits.

Bringing Fashion to Tech

  • Look great with the Lockit accessorizing your purse
  • Wear it your way with affordable designs
  • The Lockit is another way to express yourself and your style
  • Match your Lockit with different purses and outfits, and be alerted if your smartphone is left behind

How it Works?

  • The Lockit uses Bluetooth Low Energy technology and creates a virtual leash with your smartphone
  • "BEEP! BEEP! BEEP!" The Lockit alarms if your smartphone is left behind
  • Can’t find your smartphone - maybe lost in your purse? Shake the Lockit and your phone alarms
  • Wear the Lockit on your misplaced keys and page them from the smartphone app
